Work Day Scheduler

A simple calendar app for scheduling your work day

Past times are gray.
The current hour is red.
Future times are green.

Modify your calendar with the buttons to the right of each hour's text area. Save your work events with the blue save button. To change an event, simply modify the text and 'save' again. Delete events from a specific hour with the 'x' button. Clear your whole day using the button above.
